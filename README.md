## Features

- Verified onboarding — phone OTP + selfie, approved by the academy before access
- Private notation library — lesson materials visible only to the assigned student
- Live 1-on-1 video classes with your mentor, no third-party apps needed
- Class tracking — mentors see full session history per student
- Academy announcements — broadcast to all members instantly
- Community feed for all verified academy members
- In-app tutorial videos from the official GMA channel

## The Academy

**Geetanjali Music Academy** is a prestigious institution in **Shibpur, Howrah, West Bengal**, founded by **Badrinath Chatterjee**. Its mentors are All India Radio certified musicians teaching Classical, Bengali, Bollywood, and Western music — on instruments including Mandolin, Guitar, Piano, Tabla, Vocals, and Hawaiian Guitar.

This app was built to give the academy a private, dedicated digital home.
